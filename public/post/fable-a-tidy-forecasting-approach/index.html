fable - A tidy forecasting approach I

Last updated on Jul 11, 2019 1 min read

Hi, I recently stumbled upon the new fable package by Rob J Hyndman et al, which does a lot of useful things. I am going to explore this package and make a comparison with the forecast, which is a very neat package as well. The fable package is a more modern approach to time series forecasting. Whereas we used to have time series with measurements every year, month, week or day, today we are likely to encounter measurements down to minutes or seconds. fable can also handle multiple seasonality,

We need three packages:

  • fable
  • tsibble
  • fasster
